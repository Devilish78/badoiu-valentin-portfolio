Prolusion Intelligence

Prolusion Intelligence is an AI-driven incident management platform that automates the full lifecycle of IT / security incidents. It ingests events from multiple sources, classifies and prioritizes them using NLP & machine learning, visualizes relationships and dependencies in a dynamic incident graph, and recommends or triggers mitigation steps. The system aims to reduce response time (MTTR), optimize resource allocation, and improve operational resilience.
